Ofsted?

We were most recently inspected by Ofsted in January 2017 and rated us as ‘Outstanding’.

“Staff carry out extremely detailed monitoring of children’s progress”.

“Children’s behaviour is exemplary”.

“Staff make accurate assessments of children’s learning and skilfully plan an excellent range of highly motivating activities”.

“Children are emotionally secure and build extremely close bonds with staff”.
