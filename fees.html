What are our Fees?

A deposit of £250 is payable on acceptance of a place. This deposit will be refunded once the child has left, minus any outstanding fees or payments, providing the statutory two months’ notice has been given.

The first month’s fees are due one calendar month before your child’s start date.

There are two charge bands, for an extended day or a standard day. The extended day runs from 8.00 a.m. to 6.00 p.m. The standard day runs from 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Fees include the cost of all meals and formula milk taken during the hours of the charge band.

Fees will be invoiced monthly and are payable in advance by direct debit on the first day of each month. No refunds are given in the event of sickness or family holidays, as places are held open.

The minimum number of sessions is two full days per week.

We are open for 51 weeks of the year but are closed on bank holidays, for one week at Christmas and for three staff training days during the year. The charge for 51 weeks is divided equally between 12 monthly payments. Extra one-off days can occasionally be booked at a daily rate of £70. Collection of children after the correct time for the session attended will incur a penalty charge.

We participate in the Government’s Nursery Education Grant Funding which enables parents of three and four year olds to receive a contribution towards nursery fees. This funding starts on the date equivalent to the term after the child’s third birthday.

We accept part-payments from parents’ work-based childcare voucher schemes, Childcare vouchers allow working parents to exchange a proportion of their salary for vouchers before their usual tax and NI contributions are taken.

Please make cheques payable to Radius Educational Services Ltd. Card payments incur an administration fee.
