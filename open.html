When are we open?

We are open from 8.00am – 6.00pm Monday to Friday, 51 weeks of the year.

We are closed on Bank Holidays, for one week at Christmas and for three staff training days during the year.

We offer full day sessions. There are two charge bands, extended day and standard day. The extended day runs from 8.00 a.m. to 6.00 p.m. The standard day runs from 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. Fees include the cost of all meals and formula milk taken during the hours of the charge band. The charge for 51 weeks is divided equally between 12 monthly payments. Extra one-off days can occasionally be booked at a daily rate of £80.

The minimum number of sessions is two full days per week.

St. Patrick’s is situated in Cornwall Road, SE1, a stone’s throw from Waterloo Station, making it the ideal location for parents commuting into the city.
